    Christmas at Chatsworth

    It's all at Chatsworth this Christmas with so much to see and do in the house, garden and farmyard, including our exciting calendar of Christmas events and activities.

    Christmas market

    16 November 2018–4 December 2018

    Soak up the Christmas market atmosphere as you browse more than 100 stalls offering a wide range of Christmas gifts and decorations – you’ll be sure to find perfect presents for all the family.

    Please be aware that after rain the past couple days, areas of our car park are very muddy. We recommend wearing wellies or walking boots.

    Parking is included with all online bookings for Christmas in the house.

    Please be aware that after rain the past few days, the ground is very muddy in many areas of our car parks. We strongly recommend that you wear wellies or walking boots as well as warm, waterproof clothing and take care walking to and from your car.

    Where better to start your Christmas shopping than at the Chatsworth Christmas market? With over 100 market stalls offering a wide range of Christmas gifts and decorations, it's a great way to start the festive season. Enjoy a visit later in the day and experience the market at dusk with a magical feel.

    You can also explore our own exclusive ranges in the Stables shop with a wide range of home and fashion gifts, and lots of lovely stocking fillers.

    Visit by bus

    In addition to the usual bus services to Chatsworth, we've partnered with local bus companies to put on extra services during the market, between Burton and Chatsworth and Buxton and Chatsworth. View bus services to Chatsworth.

    We are fully booked for coaches during the Christmas market. We cannot accept any unbooked coaches, even for drop off or pick up.

    Further information

    The Christmas market is one of our most popular events; this means that we are constantly working to ensure the best possible experience for our visitors, in what can sometimes be challenging weather and parking conditions. This year we have made the following changes to help improve visitor experience, and minimise the impact on local residents:

    • Working with local bus companies to put on extra services during the market, in addition to those already in place, to help reduce the volume of traffic both on the estate and local roads. Learn more.
    • Introduced a new pricing structure for entry in to the market for those travelling by car, aimed at reducing the volume of traffic at peak periods, and encouraging people to car share where possible. As before, for those travelling on public buses, there is no charge for entry to the market.
    • Improved traffic management plan, including additional vehicle entry/exit points, and new parking areas with improved access to areas of ground with better conditions for parking and better drainage if the weather becomes inclement.
    • Additional lighting along the routes to and from the car parking areas across the park.
    • More shuttles to ferry visitors between the carpark and market - two tractor trailer rides, additional buggies, and a mini bus on weekends.
    • Improved walk ways and signed routes from the parking areas to the markets.
    • Extra staff in parking areas to help the traffic flow, parking, and to help our visitors.
    • A Christmas market car park map will be available from early November, and onsite during the event. The map will show the entrances to Chatsworth for the market, the different parking areas, shuttle stops and the location of the house and market. View the car park map.

    As always, parking is included with all advance online bookings to visit our Once Upon a Time themed Christmas celebration in the house. No additional charges apply for bookings made online in advance. Learn more about Christmas at Chatsworth.
